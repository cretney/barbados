NOTES

A.
For the purpose of the qualification of origin of the products which Venezuela will exempt from import duties and restrictions. The products must be:

i) Wholly produced in Barbados or any CARICOM MemberCountry.

(ii) Items manufactured using materials from third countries provided that they result from a process of substantial transformation carried out in the territory of Caricom Member Countries. which creates a different product characterized by the fact of being classified within the Harmonized Commodity Description and Coding System in a heading different from that of such materials.

(iii) items that are a result of assembly operations which constitutes a process of substantial transformation, carried out in a Caricom Member Country and in the production of which materials from third countries including Venezuela are used, when the % value of the materials from third countries does not exceed 50% of the FOB export value of the finished product.

iv) items which comply to the specific origin requirements established in Appendix 2 of the agreement between CARICOM and the Republic of Venezuela.

B.
Sanctions. Persons who fumish, or cause to be fumished, information which relates to origin or consignment, and which is untrue in a material particular are liable to legal penalties and to the suspension of facilities for their goods to obtain preference.
