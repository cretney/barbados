C.    PROCEDURE FOR CLAIMING/COMMON MARKET/COMMUNITY/ORIGIN

A declaration on the certificate of origin form must be prepared by the exporters of the goods and submitted together with a Common Market Origin application form to the certifying authority of the country of exportation which will, if satisfied, certify the certificate of origin and return it to the exporter for transmission to the importer in the country of destination. The certifying authority will itself retain the Common Market origin application form duly completed and signed by the exporter.

D.    SANCTIONS

Persons who furnish or cause to be furnished untrue declarations render themselves liable to penalties.
